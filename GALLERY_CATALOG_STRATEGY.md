# Gallery Catalog Strategy

## Purpose

This file explains how the Viral Prints gallery and curated image catalog should be structured.

The goal is to help customers get inspired and choose a design direction even if they do not already have artwork.

---

## Gallery Goals

The gallery should:
- inspire customers
- help them imagine possibilities
- reduce uncertainty
- make browsing easy
- support quote conversion

---

## Initial Gallery Approach

In Phase 1, the gallery can use:
- curated stock imagery
- concept visuals
- categorized inspiration by industry and style

The gallery does not need to depend on a large existing project portfolio at launch.

---

### By Style

### By Mood or Use Case
Examples:

- premium atmosphere
- community-focused
- high-energy

---

## Catalog Purpose in the Buying Flow

The catalog should support customers who:
- do not have artwork ready
- need inspiration
- want a faster path than custom design
- need help choosing a style direction

---

## Phase 1 Recommendation

Phase 1 should keep the gallery simple and organized.

Use:
- stock images
- concept images
- categorized examples
- clear CTA to Start Project

---

## Future Gallery Possibilities

Later, the gallery may include:
- customer case studies
- before and after examples
- filterable portfolio results
- industry-specific design packs
- stronger ROI and engagement examples
