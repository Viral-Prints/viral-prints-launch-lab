# About Viral Prints

Viral Prints is a creative art business focused on making large-scale wall art and mural-style visual experiences more accessible and affordable for businesses.

The company is designed to help transform blank walls into visually engaging spaces that strengthen branding, customer experience, and community identity.

Viral Prints sits at the intersection of creativity, business, and technology. The long-term vision includes building a streamlined customer experience that makes it easier for businesses to explore concepts, request quotes, preview projects, and move from idea to installation.

This repository supports the early-stage buildout of the business, including website planning, customer journey design, quote workflows, CRM systems, mockup concepts, and related operational tools.
