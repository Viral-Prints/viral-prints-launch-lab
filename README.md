# Viral Prints Launch Lab

This repository supports the early-stage buildout of Viral Prints.

Viral Prints is a creative art business focused on making large-scale wall art and mural experiences more accessible and affordable for businesses.

This project is designed to help shape the business through collaboration in areas such as:

- website design
- quote request flows
- customer journey design
- CRM and operational workflows
- product mockups and visualization concepts
- brand storytelling and digital content

## Who this project is for

We welcome contributors who are interested in:

- UX/UI design
- web development
- startup systems
- CRM and workflow design
- marketing and storytelling
- product thinking
