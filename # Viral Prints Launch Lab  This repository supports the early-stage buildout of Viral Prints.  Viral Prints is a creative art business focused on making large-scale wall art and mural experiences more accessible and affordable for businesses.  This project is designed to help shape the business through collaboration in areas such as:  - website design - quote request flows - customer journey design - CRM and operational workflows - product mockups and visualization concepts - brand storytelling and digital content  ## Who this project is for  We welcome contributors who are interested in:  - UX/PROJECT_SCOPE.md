# Project Scope

## Project Name
Viral Prints Launch Lab

## Purpose
This project supports the early-stage design and operational buildout of Viral Prints.

## Goal
Create a strong digital and operational foundation for the business by improving:

- website structure
- customer journey
- quote request flow
- CRM and workflow systems
- product visualization concepts
- brand messaging

## In Scope
- website mockups
- page structure recommendations
- quote request flow design
- customer journey mapping
- CRM workflow recommendations
- content and messaging improvements
- market research support

## Out of Scope for Phase 1
- full production website build
- advanced custom software development
- full automation deployment
- enterprise-level CRM migration

## Deliverables
- website mockups
- quote flow recommendations
- CRM workflow outline
- customer journey map
- brand and messaging guidance
- issue backlog for contributors
