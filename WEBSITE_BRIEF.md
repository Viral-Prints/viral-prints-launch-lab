# Website Brief

## Goal
Design a clean, modern, conversion-friendly website for Viral Prints.

## Core Website Pages
- Home
- About
- Services
- Portfolio / Examples
- Request a Quote

## Main Website Goals
The website should help visitors:

- understand what Viral Prints does
- see the value of the service
- view examples or concepts
- request a quote easily
- trust the brand

## Desired Feel
- clean
- modern
- visual
- creative
- professional
- easy to navigate

## Key Calls to Action
- Request a Quote
- View Our Work
- Learn More
- Book a Consultation

## Important Sections
### Home
- hero section
- what we do
- who it is for
- process overview
- featured examples
- call to action

### About
- business story
- mission
- why Viral Prints exists
- vision for community and business impact

### Services
- wall printing or mural offering
- process
- types of spaces served
- pricing or quote prompt

### Portfolio / Examples
- concept examples
- finished project examples
- categories by business type

### Request a Quote
- intake form
- project details
- business information
- timeline
- contact information
